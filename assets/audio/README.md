# Audio Assets

Place your royalty-free audio files here:

1. **calm.mp3** - Calming meditation music (5-10 minutes)
2. **breathing.mp3** - Guided breathing exercise (3-5 minutes)
3. **nature.mp3** - Nature sounds like rain, ocean waves, or forest (5-10 minutes)

## Free Audio Sources:

### Recommended Sites:
- **Pixabay Audio**: https://pixabay.com/music/
  - 100% free, no attribution required
  - Search for: "meditation", "calm", "nature sounds"

- **Free Music Archive**: https://freemusicarchive.org/
  - Filter by "Public Domain" or "CC0" licenses

- **YouTube Audio Library**: https://www.youtube.com/audiolibrary
  - Free music and sound effects
  - Download and convert to MP3

- **Freesound**: https://freesound.org/
  - Search for: "rain", "ocean", "forest", "meditation"

### Audio Specifications:
- Format: MP3
- Bitrate: 128-192 kbps (keeps file size reasonable)
- Duration: 3-10 minutes
- Volume: Normalized (not too loud)

### Example Searches:
- "calm piano meditation"
- "rain sounds"
- "ocean waves"
- "forest ambience"
- "breathing exercise"
- "relaxation music"

## Note:
The app will work without audio files, but the audio player feature will not function until you add the MP3 files here.
